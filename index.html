About MetLab

MetLab Testing and Analysis Services is a proudly Qatari-owned materials testing laboratory. We specialize in Corrosion Analysis, Mechanical testing, coating Analysis, and Metallurgical Assesment.

Our operations align with the Qatar National Vision 2030, contributing to industrial self-reliance, safety, and sustainability goals.

Driven by precision, integrity, and technical excellence, MetLab is a trusted partner to oil & gas, petrochemical, marine, and manufacturing industries.

Testing Capabilities

Mechanical Testing

  • Tensile Testing
  • Bend Testing (3-Point / 4-Point)
  • Charpy Impact Testing
  • Fracture Toughness (K_IC, J-Integral)
  • Hardness Tests (Rockwell, Vickers, Brinell)
  • Micro Hardness Testing
  • Fatigue & Cyclic Loading
  • Compression Test
  • Shear Test
  • Through Thickness Tensile (Z-Tensile)

Metallurgical Analysis

  • Microstructure Examination
  • Macro Examination
  • Grain Size Analysis
  • Prior Austenitic Grain Size (Carburizing & Oxidation Method)
  • Failure Analysis & Fractography
  • Inclusion & Defect Analysis
  • Phase Analysis
  • SEM & EDS Analysis

Corrosion Testing

  • Salt Spray / Fog Test
  • Electrochemical Testing (Potentiodynamic Polarization, EIS)
  • Stress Corrosion Cracking (SCC)
  • Pitting and Crevice Corrosion Tests
  • Intergranular Corrosion (IGC)
  • Hydrogen Induced Cracking (HIC)
  • Immersion / Chemical Exposure Tests
  • Accelerated Environmental Corrosion Simulation
  • Galvanic Corrosion Studies

Coating Analysis

  • Coating Thickness Measurement
  • Adhesion & Pull-off Tests
  • Hardness & Abrasion Resistance Tests

  • Surface Roughness & Profile Analysis
  • Flexibility Testing
  • Cathodic Disbondment
  • Differential Scanning Calorimetry (DSC)
  • Peel Testing
  • Hot Water Soak
  • Visual & Microscopic Coating Inspection
