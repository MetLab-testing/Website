About MetLab

MetLab Testing and Analysis Services is a proudly Qatari-owned materials testing laboratory. We specialize in Corrosion Analysis, Mechanical testing, coating Analysis, and Metallurgical Assesment.

Our operations align with the Qatar National Vision 2030, contributing to industrial self-reliance, safety, and sustainability goals.

Driven by precision, integrity, and technical excellence, MetLab is a trusted partner to oil & gas, petrochemical, marine, and manufacturing industries.

  • Bend Testing (3-Point / 4-Point)
  • Charpy Impact Testing
  • Fracture Toughness (K_IC, J-Integral)

  • Micro Hardness Testing
  • Fatigue & Cyclic Loading
  • Compression Test

  • Microstructure Examination
